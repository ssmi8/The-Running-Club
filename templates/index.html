Welcome to The Running Club

The Running Club is more than just a running club. The Running Club is focussed around inclusion, strong values and ever-lasting friendship. No matter your ability, there is a place for you here. We make sure you never run alone and will always give you the support and guidance to make you a better and stonger runner. Whether you are an experienced runner, picking back up from an injury or spell away from running, or even if you are running for the first time in a group, The Running Club allows you to run at your own pace, and will encourage you and push you to new heights! So, what are you waiting for? Strap on those running shoes, and let's get going!
